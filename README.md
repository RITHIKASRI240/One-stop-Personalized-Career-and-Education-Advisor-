**One-Stop Personalized Career and Education Advisor**

**PROBLEM STATEMENT**

**Many students face challenges in choosing the right career path and educational opportunities due to a lack of personalized guidance and reliable information. Career-related resources, educational options, and skill development opportunities are often spread across multiple platforms, making decision-making difficult. This project aims to develop a centralized platform that provides personalized career recommendations, educational guidance, and skill development suggestions based on users' interests, qualifications, and career goals.**
